OUR STORY:
THE BEST OF BOTH WORLDS

We are a newly founded, local Berkshire business with a fresh approach to customer service. But our foundation is built on rock-solid expertise.

Twin Phase Electrical was started with a simple mission: to bring trustworthy, high-quality, and transparent electrical services to our community. We are founded on over 10+ years of hands-on, industry experience, covering everything from complex commercial rewires to domestic emergency callouts.

Our qualifications are your guarantee of safe, compliant, and professional workmanship.
